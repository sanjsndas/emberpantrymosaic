Privacy Policy

Your trust is the secret ingredient. We handle your data with the same care we use for a slow‑fermented sourdough — transparent, respectful, and never exploited.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect only what you voluntarily give us: name, email address, and any message you send via our contact form or newsletter sign‑up. We also gather minimal anonymous usage data to improve your experience (e.g., which pages are most loved).

2. How we use your data

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages.
  • Send you our Mosaic Letter (only if you opt in).
  • Improve our content and recipe recommendations.
  • Never, ever sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data security

We use industry‑standard measures (SSL encryption, secure servers) to protect your data. Access is limited to essential team members who are bound by confidentiality.

4. Third‑party services

We may use trusted platforms like email providers or analytics tools. These services are contractually obligated to keep your data safe and only process it on our behalf.

5. Your rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to update or correct inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt‑out: Unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in our messages.

6. Children’s privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will remove it.

7. Changes to this policy

We’ll update this page as our practices evolve. Significant changes will be highlighted on our website or via email. The “last updated” date at the top indicates the latest revision.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions about privacy or your data, please reach out at privacy@emberpantry.com or use our contact form. We’re here to help.
