    Frequently asked questions

    What’s the best flour for sourdough?

    Bread flour or a mix of whole wheat and strong white flour. Freshly milled grains add incredible depth.

    How do I keep my starter healthy?

    Feed regularly, store at room temp or fridge, and use unchlorinated water. A consistent feeding schedule builds strength.

    Can I ferment without salt?

    Salt controls undesirable bacteria and improves texture. For salt‑free ferments, consider using whey or a starter culture.
